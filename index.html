FORTRESS Gaming Technologies S.A. (FGT S.A.) was founded as an R&D unit of NOVOMATIC Group of Companies – an Austrian gaming and entertainment leader with a global reach. FGT S.A. specializes in the development of lottery management systems and applications. Since the beginning, the company has placed an emphasis on the development and implementation of new technologies, which allows it to retain a strong position on the very competitive market.

NOVOMATIC Group of Companies currently employs about 20 000 employees and generates an annual revenue of over 3.5 billion euro.

Creative workspace

The company office is located in a revitalized 19th century fort. Its modern interior is enclosed within a distinctive tube shape which reflects the historical purpose of the building. Each team has a separate, well-equipped room. A part of the observation deck is used as a terrace for our BBQ get-togethers.
